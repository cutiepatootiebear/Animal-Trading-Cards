Chesty the bull dog

Since 1922, the United States Marine Corps has used Bulldogs as its mascots.

  • fact # 1: U.S. Marine Maj. Gen. Smedley Butler introduced the first Marine mascot, named "Pvt Jiggs,"
  • fact # 2: The current mascot is the 16th in a series of mascots named "Chesty" in honor of famed Marine Lieutenant General Lewis B. "Chesty" Puller Jr.
  • fact # 3: This dog lives at the Marine Barracks, Washington, D.C.
  • fact # 4: This dog appears in weekly parades.
